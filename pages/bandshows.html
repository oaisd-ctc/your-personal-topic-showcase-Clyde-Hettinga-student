2021
SPIRITS of the CANYON

Overview

Parts:

  1. Sunrise
    • Music: "Grand Canyon Fanfare" - James Newton Howard
    • Started slowly and quietly, with one guard member as the sun "waking up" the canyon
    • Had French Horn feature, became lively/triumphant shortly after, and remained as such
    • Guard danced and used 6-foot flags with the canyon design
  2. Daytime
    • Music: "Joy" from Awakening - Joseph Curiale
    • Lively and bold for the entire part
    • Guard had a saber feature and used 6-foot flags with a lizard design
    • Some flags got on props
  3. The River
    • Music: "Yosemite Autumn" - Mark Camphouse
    • This was our ballad
    • A prop was opened to reveal a waterfall, and two people brought out the river (fabric)
    • Guard danced and used double swing flags with the water design
  4. Nighttime
    • Music: "Ghost Dances" - Roland Barrett
    • Mostly sinister and mildly chaotic for the first half, bold for the second half
    • Other closed props were opened to reveal cave paintings
    • Guard had their costume change and a rifle feature, some came out of the cave painting props, and used 6-foot flags with night sky designs
    • "Sun" guard member came back out at the end
    • We had a fake ending, after which the music calmed down and we clustered at the props

2022
HEART of the JUNGLE

Overview

Parts:

  1. Welcome
    • Music: "Pandora" - James Cameron
    • There was supposed to be a preshow, but we never did that
    • Bold for the entire part
    • Guard danced and used swing flags with a jungle leaf design
  2. The Flora
    • Music: "Danzon No. 2" - Arturo Márquez
    • Started almost as a ballad, but became almost opposite of that very quickly and remained as such
    • Guard continued using the swing flags, had a saber feature, and then used 6-foot flags with a Bird of Paradise design
    • Sabers and 6-foots had partner work
  3. The Fauna
    • Music: Original composition
    • Every animal had a different theme, so each section of this part had a different feel
    • Ending combined all of the animal themes
    • Guard had their costume change
    • Snakes used long 6-foot flags with a snake design that attached to each other
    • Leopards were the rifle feature
    • Frogs used translucent 6-foot flags with a poison dart frog design
    • Birds used double swing flags with a parrot wing design
    • At the end, all guard used 6-foot flags with every animal design
  4. The Heart of the Jungle
    • Music: "Jake's First Flight" - James Cameron
    • This was our ballad up until the end, which was bold and triumphant
    • Guard continued using the 6-foots, then swing flags with leaf designs over a blue-and-gold background
    • Panel props were opened to reveal a landscape

DECO

Overview

Deco didn't have unique themes for each part like the other shows on this page, but each one did have some stylistic differences:

  1. Part 1:
    • Music: "Rhapsody in Blue" - George Gershwin
    • If you don't know Rhapsody in Blue, this part was mostly bold
    • Guard used swing flags with orange, black, and silver geometric designs, had a saber feature, and ended with 6-foot flags with a blue-and-gold geometric design
  2. Part 2:
    • Music: "An American in Paris" - George Gershwin
    • This was our ballad
    • Guard changed into skirts and used swing flags with a lady's face overtop a blue background
